## Gap Analysis

### Top Identified Gaps

#### Gap 1: Quality rhythm games (high opportunity)
The Funky Friday incumbent (rank 19, 4 years old) is showing flat engagement. DevForum posts requesting "new rhythm game" or "better Funky Friday" are frequent. The genre has high appeal but only one significant competitor.

#### Gap 2: Accessible fighting RPGs (high opportunity)
Type Soul and Wisteria (both trending up) serve the anime-inspired fighting RPG niche but have steep learning curves noted in player comments. A fighting RPG with a better tutorial and cleaner progression could capture the same audience.

#### Gap 3: Cozy survival (medium opportunity)
No top-20 survival game exists. Multiple DevForum threads request a "cozy survival" game. The mechanic gap is specifically: survival without griefing (solo or co-op PvE). The minecraft-style survival niche has minimal good execution on Roblox.
